TodoList Docklet for ObjectDock
(c) 2004 Julien TEMPLIER

Parts from Weather Docklet by Jeff Bargmann


The TodoList docklet allows you to add tasks to your dock,set priorities
for them, add a due date and be reminded when the task is overdue.
